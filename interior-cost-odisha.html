What affects interior design cost in Odisha?

Project cost depends on location, size, finishes, complexity of joinery, lighting & electrical scope, and whether structural works are needed. Elysian Designs offers three clear packages — Base, Premium and Luxury — to help you choose.

Residential pricing (how we calculate)

For homes — Elysian's standard approach is:

  • Base package: Starting at ₹3,00,000 + ₹50,000 per room (GST applicable).
  • Premium package: Upgraded finishes, veneer/PU, improved lighting and branded fixtures.
  • Luxury package: Bespoke joinery, designer finishes, imported materials and architectural lighting.

What is included in a site visit?

Measurement verification, client brief, scope definition and initial budget discussion. Site visits are chargeable but often credited against the final contract.

How long does a typical interior project take?

Residential projects (moderate scope) generally take 6–10 weeks; premium/luxury or structural projects take longer depending on scope and material lead times.
